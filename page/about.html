Minami Strings

About

Minami Strings is a part of the music society of Yokohama Municipal Minami High School and the Junior High School Attached to Minami High School. In former days, the school's music society consisted of 4 clubs: Chorus, Brass Band, Guitar and Mandolin Club, and Strings. However, Brass Band and Strings are currently active today. We enjoy, endeavour, and collaborate with everyone who loves music. If you want to get more detailed information, please find and ask Maestro GANTA strolling around the school.
