School Festival Concert 2018

8 Sep. 2018

Minami Strings held a concert in our School Festival (南高祭, Nanko-Sai).

This concert is the first stage for the first-year members, and for the second-year members, the final performance of the School Festival. The whole 35 members put their hearts into the performance. Please, by all means, drop into our concert when you come to the School Festival.
