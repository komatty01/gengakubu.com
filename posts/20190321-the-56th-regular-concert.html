The 56th Regular Concert

21 Mar. 2019

Minami Strings held the 56th Regular Concert with Minami Winds(Brass Band) on 21 March 2019.
The whole members practiced so hard for this concert that you should not miss it. Additionally, Strings and Winds played together as an orchestra.

Concert Information

Date 21 Mar. 2019
Open 13:00
Performance 13:30–
Place Kannai Hall
Programme
  • C-M. Schönberg: Selections from 'Les Misérables'
  • J. Sibelius: Finlandia, etc.

Thank you very much for your coming to the concert.

Know more about this concert on our Twitter (@y_minamistrings).
